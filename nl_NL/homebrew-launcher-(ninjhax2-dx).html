    Voor hulp in het Engels kan je terecht bij Nintendo Homebrew op Discord.

    Technical Details (optional)

    Ninjhax2 is an exploit for the game “Cubic Ninja” made by smealum. This page uses an updated version of ninjhax by zoogie called ninjhax2-dx, which supports 11.17.0.

    This method requires a working 3DS camera.

    This process will overwrite your Cubic Ninja save file, if you have one.

    Wat je nodig hebt

    • The game “Cubic Ninja”
    • The latest release of ninjhax2-dx
    • De nieuwste versie van Luma3DS (het Luma3DS .zip bestand)

    Instructies

    Deel I - Voorbereiding

    1. Zet je console uit
    2. Plaats je SD kaart in je computer
    3. Copy Launcher.dat from the folder appropriate for your console from the ninjhax2-dx .zip file to the root of your SD card
      • De hoofdmap van de SD-kaart verwijst naar de bovenste map van de SD-kaart, waarin je alle mappen op de SD-kaart kunt zien, waaronder de Nintendo 3DS map, zonder je in de Nintendo 3DS map te bevinden
    4. Kopieer boot.firm en boot.3dsx van de Luma3DS .zip naar de hoofdmap van je SD-kaart

    Section II - ninjhax2-dx

    1. Stop je SD-kaart terug in je console
    2. Zet je console aan
    3. Launch “Cubic Ninja” once and select “Create”
      • If prompted, delete all data
    4. Select “QR Code”
    5. Select “Scan QR Code”
    6. On your PC, open the browser and go to ninjhax2-dx website
    7. Select your console’s region and hardware
    8. On your console, scan the QR code
      • If the game exits to the HOME Menu, then the exploit has installed correctly
    9. Launch “Cubic Ninja” again
    10. If the exploit was successful, your console will have booted into the Homebrew Launcher
    11. Zet je console uit

    Section III - Hardware Button Check

    In this section, you will see whether you can access Safe Mode on your console. This will determine which method you will follow on the next page.

    In this section, you will see whether your shoulder buttons are working on your console. This will determine which method you will follow on the next page.

    1. Zet je console aan
    2. Once you see the HOME Menu, press the (Left Shoulder) and (Right Shoulder) buttons at the same time
      • The camera applet should appear
    3. Zet je console uit

    Next step: Choose an exploit

    If the camera appeared in the previous section, Safe Mode is likely to be working on your console.

    If the camera appeared, continue to Installing boot9strap (HBL-USM)

    If the camera did NOT appear, continue to Installing boot9strap (Frogtool)
